NOTES:
  • X increases horizotally from left to right, Y increases vertically from top to bottom. 1 unit = 1 cm = 0.01 m.
  • Total usable area = [-100 m, 100 m)2, divided into 400 blocks of dimensions 10 m x 10 m (useful for developer).
  • In "Navigation" mode, scroll to zoom in/out and drag to pan. Use "HOME" button to undo all navigations. Navigation does not change coordinates of actual waypoints/nodes.
  • In "WayPoints/Nodes" mode, double click to add and (Shift + Double Click) to remove a waypoint.
  • In "Edges" mode, click on two nodes one by one to add an edge (no effect for pre-existing edge). Use Shift while clicking one/both nodes to delete an edge (no effect for non-existing edge).
  • Edge weight = edge length / speed. Average edge length should be 0.5 m (50 units). Average speed should be 10 m/s (36 kmph).
